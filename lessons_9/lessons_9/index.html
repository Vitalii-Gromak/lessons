Icons are small, simple images that enhance the effect of the text, or carry certain information on their own. In most cases, these are vector images (SVG). Icons can be integrated both with the IMG tag and through CSS (background and others).

Content images - images that are the content of the site. In most cases, these are raster images (photos) (JPG/PNG -> WEBP). All content images are output with the IMG tag.
